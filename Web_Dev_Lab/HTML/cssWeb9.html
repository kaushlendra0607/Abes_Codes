Beaches

Introduction
Beaches are one of the most popular natural places in the world. They are formed along the edges of oceans and seas. A beach usually has sand, waves, shells, and sunlight.
Why People Love Beaches
Many people enjoy beaches because the sound of the waves is relaxing. The cool breeze and open sky make it a peaceful place to visit. Beaches are also known for activities like:
  • Swimming
  • Walking on sand
  • Collecting shells
  • Watching sunsets
